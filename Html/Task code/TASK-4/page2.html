Welcome to India

A Land of Diversity

India is a country in South Asia known for its rich culture, history, and traditions. It is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous country in the world.

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: New Delhi
  • Official Languages: Hindi, English, and many regional languages
  • Famous Monument: Taj Mahal
  • Independence Day: 15th August 1947
