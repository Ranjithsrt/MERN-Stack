TIGER

About Tigers

Tigers (Panthera tigris) are the largest cat species in the world. They are known for their striking orange coats with black stripes and are primarily found in Asia. Tigers are apex predators and play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystems.

Diet

Tigers are carnivores, and their diet mainly consists of large prey such as deer, wild boar, and buffalo. They are powerful hunters with sharp teeth and strong jaws.
